FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to questions I frequently get asked about working together.

What's your typical project timeline?

+

Most projects range from 4-12 weeks depending on scope. I typically allocate 1-2 weeks for discovery and planning, followed by iterative development sprints. For smaller features or prototypes, we can move faster. I always provide realistic timelines upfront and keep you updated throughout.

Do you work with agencies or just direct clients?

+

Both! I partner with design agencies, dev shops, and direct clients. I'm comfortable white-labeling work, joining existing teams, or leading projects independently. What matters most is good collaboration and clear communication.

What's your design-to-development process?

+

I work closely with designers (or can handle design myself for certain projects). I prefer Figma handoffs with proper design systems, but I'm flexible. I focus on translating static designs into living, breathing experiences with thoughtful motion and interaction.

What technologies do you primarily use?

+

React, Next.js, TypeScript, and modern animation libraries (GSAP, Framer Motion, Three.js). For styling: Tailwind, CSS-in-JS, or CSS Modules. I'm framework-agnostic and can adapt to your stack. Backend? I work with Node/Express but prefer to partner with backend specialists for complex systems.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

+

I build in regular review checkpoints and love iterative feedback. Most projects include 2-3 revision rounds per phase. I use Loom videos, staging deployments, and collaborative tools to make feedback seamless. The goal is to get to the best solution together.

What's your availability like?

+

I typically work with 2-3 clients simultaneously. Currently booking for Q2 2026 and beyond. I'm based in EST but work with clients globally. I'm flexible on meeting times and always responsive within 24 hours during business days.
